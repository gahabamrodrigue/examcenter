IELTS Progress Check

What is IELTS Progress Check?

IELTS Progress Check is an official IELTS online paid practice test. Choose between IELTS Academic or IELTS General Training practice tests. 

Completed tests are marked by IELTS trained and qualified markers. Within 5 days, you will receive an official feedback report, including an indicative overall test band score as well as a band score for each section of the test (Listening, Reading, Writing & Speaking).

Benefits of IELTS Progress Check

Official online practice test          

Practise on official IELTS questions online at a time convenient for you. IELTS Progress Check is as close to the real test as possible, getting you ready for test day.

Feedback and Indicative Band Score

Marked by IELTS experts, within 5 days receive an official feedback report including an indicative band score to help you know where to improve.

Choose the practice test right for you

Available to purchase in timed conditions like test day or untimed to give you more flexibility. Choose from a range of tests for IELTS Academic and IELTS General Training.
