Reach out to test takers

Organisations that accept IELTS results include government departments, educational institutions, professional associations and employers. Results are used for immigration, education and professional accreditation and employment purposes.

The IELTS website lists thousands of these organisations from all around the world.

Reach out to test takers and compare band score requirements of other institutions by listing your organisation's URL and minimum band score requirements on the IELTS website. Our website receives about ten million visitors every year. Let the millions of people who take the test know that you accept IELTS scores.

Check whether your organisation is listed and that your required test scores are correct.

Register your organisation's details on IELTS.org.

For organisation in the USA, please find more information on IELTS in the USA on our website. To download a list of all organisations that use IELTS results in the USA, view the complimentary resources for stakeholders.
