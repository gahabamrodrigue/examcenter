How the IELTS test can help you meet your organisation's need

  • choose from an Academic and a General Training test
  • available up to four times a month
  • 1,600 test locations in over 140 countries (view worldwide list)
  • test cost is set locally with payment in local currency
  • results are issued to test takers 13 calendar days after test
  • Test Report Forms are sent directly to your organisation (mail or electronic download) – if nominated by the test taker
  • a free Results Verification Service is available – more fully explained in Chapter 2.
