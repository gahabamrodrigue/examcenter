Four Skills

Each section in this chapter takes about 15-20 min to complete

This chapter provides examples of IELTS test questions and authentic samples of test taker responses so you can better understand what level of proficiency is required to achieve a certain band score level.

You can then check this against the level of proficiency required within your own setting to ensure you have your requirements set at the appropriate level.

You will also become familiar with the format of each of the four skills tested and see how they are assessed and marked.
