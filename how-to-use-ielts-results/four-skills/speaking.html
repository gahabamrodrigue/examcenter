Speaking test

This topic looks at the distinctive IELTS face-to-face Speaking test. This test is the same for both the Academic and General Training tests.

Objectives of this topic:

  1. understand the format of the Speaking test
  2. understand the criteria on which the Speaking test is marked by becoming familiar with the public version of the IELTS assessment criteria
  3. listen to parts of the test and understand the speaking proficiency required to achieve a certain band score level
