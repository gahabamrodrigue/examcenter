    • Test takers

    • Send your Application Form (PDF, 329KB) to the centre at which you intend to take IELTS.
    • Contact your test centre for most enquiries, including test dates, fees and enquiries on results.
    • Download the Information for Candidates booklet (PDF, 1,021KB) and order the IELTS Official Practice Materials.
    • Search for organisations that accept IELTS using the "Who accepts IELTS scores" search tool.
    • Practise sample test questions.
    • Visit our official Facebook page.

    Organisations

    • Add your organisation to the "Who accepts IELTS scores" search tool. You can also request changes to your organisation's information.

    • Download various IELTS publications.
    • Order IELTS Research Reports.
    • View test performance data.
    • Visit IELTS News for all the latest news and announcements.
    • See our test locations.
