Processing and verifying IELTS results

The IELTS Results Verification Service enables organisations to check, securely and free of charge, whether test takers are presenting genuine results.

Get started with the IELTS Verification Service

Use our guide for advice on how to register and use the IELTS Results Verification Service. The IELTS Results Verification Service enables organisations to check whether test takers are presenting genuine results. This service is free of charge.

For all recognising organisations (excluding Canada and the United States)

Organisations can also opt to download results, instead of receiving hard copies of individual Test Report Forms. Please note that this service is available to all IELTS recognising organisations (excluding those in Canada and the United States).

IELTS Results Service for Canada and the United States only

The IELTS Results Service enables organisations in Canada and the United States to check whether test-takers are presenting genuine results using the Verification Service and also allows organisations to receive scores electronically through the E-Delivery Service account. This service is free of charge. 

IELTS Results Service

How does IELTS test verification work?

Option 1: Verify individual IELTS Test Report Forms

The free, secure IELTS Results Verification Service allows organisations to quickly verify that an applicant is presenting a genuine Test Report Form by checking that the results you receive match the results held on the IELTS database.

Once you have registered for the IELTS Results Verification Service, you may then opt in to download IELTS results electronically instead of receiving hard copies of results.

Option 2: Securely download results electronically*

The IELTS download service allows registered organisations to securely download verified IELTS results for all test takers who have applied to their organisation. This means:

  • you can feel confident that results are genuine as they will be securely transferred direct from official IELTS systems to your data system
  • you will save time by downloading results in bulk, at a time that's convenient for you
  • test takers will no longer need to present paper Test Report Forms.

Results can be downloaded in either .XML or .CSV format.

*Please note that if you opt into the secure batch downloads service, hard copy Test Report Forms will no longer be posted to your institution.
