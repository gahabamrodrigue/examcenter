US Recognition Portal

To officially accept IELTS, we ask you to please complete the online IELTS recognition form to ensure your organization is set up to receive official IELTS scores and to gain access to IELTS complimentary services.

The form should only be completed by organizations that do not currently accept IELTS.

 

Looking for new recruitment opportunities?

Sign up to promote your institution to more than 5 million fans who follow the IELTS Official Facebook page and more than 8,500 followers on the IELTS USA LinkedIn page for free.
